Recipe #1: Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 20 minutes (prep) + 10-12 minutes (baking)
  • Serves: 2-3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade) 1 pizza crust
Fresh mozzarella cheese (sliced) 8 oz
Tomato sauce (preferably pizza sauce or marinara) 1/2 cup
Fresh basil leaves 5-6 leaves
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Salt and pepper To taste

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat.

Step 2: Roll out or stretch the pizza dough on a floured surface.

Step 3: Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.

Step 4: Place the mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce.

Step 5: Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.

Step 6: Drizzle with olive oil and top with fresh basil leaves.

Step 7: Slice and serve!

Recipe #2: Pepperoni Pizza

  • Time: 25 minutes (prep) + 10-12 minutes (baking)
  • Serves: 3-4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade) 1 pizza crust
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Shredded mozzarella cheese 8 oz
Pepperoni slices 20-30 slices
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Oregano 1 tsp
Crushed red pepper flakes (optional) To taste

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

Step 2: Roll or stretch the pizza dough onto a floured surface.

Step 3: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

Step 4: Add shredded mozzarella cheese and arrange pepperoni slices on top.

Step 5: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown.

Step 6: Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with oregano, and add red pepper flakes if desired.

Step 7: Slice and serve!

Recipe #3: Veggie Supreme Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes (prep) + 10-12 minutes (baking)
  • Serves: 3-4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade) 1 pizza crust
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Shredded mozzarella cheese 8 oz
Red onion (sliced) 1/2 onion
Bell peppers (sliced) 1/2 pepper (any color)
Olives (sliced) 1/4 cup
Mushrooms (sliced) 1/2 cup
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Italian seasoning 1 tsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

Step 2: Roll or stretch the pizza dough onto a floured surface.

Step 3: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.

Step 4: Add shredded mozzarella cheese and top with sliced red onion, bell peppers, olives, and mushrooms.

Step 5: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and the crust is golden.

Step 6: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with Italian seasoning.

Step 7: Slice and serve!
